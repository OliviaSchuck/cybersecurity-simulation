🔐 Cybersecurity Awareness Simulation

Understand your digital risk through real-world decision-making.

This simulation evaluates how you respond to common cybersecurity situations like phishing, password security, public Wi-Fi, AI privacy, and device safety.

Unlike a quiz, it measures your behavior in realistic scenarios and gives instant feedback on your choices.
