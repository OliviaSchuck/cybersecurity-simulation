🔐 Cybersecurity Awareness Simulation

Understand your digital risk through real-world decision-making.

This interactive simulation evaluates how you respond to everyday cybersecurity situations—similar to those you encounter online.

You’ll navigate scenarios involving password security, phishing attempts, public Wi-Fi, AI data privacy, and device protection.

How it works

Instead of testing memorization, this tool measures your behavioral choices in realistic situations. Each decision provides instant feedback so you can recognize secure vs. risky digital habits in real time.

Your results

At the end, you’ll receive a personalized cybersecurity risk score, a breakdown of your digital behavior patterns, and actionable recommendations to improve your online safety.
