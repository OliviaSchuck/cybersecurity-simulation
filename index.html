Cybersecurity Awareness Simulation

This interactive tool is designed to evaluate cybersecurity awareness through real-world decision-making scenarios. It focuses on common risks such as password management, phishing attempts, public Wi-Fi usage, AI data privacy, and device security.

Unlike traditional quizzes, this simulation measures behavioral choices rather than simple factual knowledge. Each response provides feedback to help users better understand secure and unsafe digital practices.

At the end of the simulation, users receive a personalized cybersecurity risk score and summary of their digital safety habits.
